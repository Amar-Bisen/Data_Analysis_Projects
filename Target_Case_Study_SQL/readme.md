# 📊 Target Brazil E-Commerce SQL Case Study

## Overview

This project analyzes Target's e-commerce operations in Brazil between **2016 and 2018** using SQL. The objective was to investigate customer behavior, order trends, logistics performance, freight costs, delivery efficiency, and payment patterns to generate actionable business recommendations.

The analysis was conducted on a dataset containing over **100,000 orders** across **4,119 cities** and **27 states** in Brazil.

---

## Business Objective

Target wants to understand:

* How e-commerce adoption evolved across Brazil.
* Customer purchasing behavior and peak ordering periods.
* State-wise operational performance.
* Logistics and delivery challenges.
* Payment preferences and revenue growth patterns.

The goal is to support strategic decisions that improve operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and market expansion.

## Key Business Insights

### 📈 E-Commerce Growth

* Order volume increased consistently throughout the analysis period.
* Brazil showed strong e-commerce adoption across multiple regions.
* No significant monthly seasonality was observed.

### 🕒 Customer Behavior

* Most orders were placed between **10 AM and 10 PM**.
* Afternoon and evening periods generated the highest transaction volumes.

### 🌎 Regional Analysis

* São Paulo (SP) had the highest number of customers and orders.
* MG, SP, and PR demonstrated steady month-over-month growth.
* Several states showed potential for customer acquisition campaigns.

### 🚚 Logistics Insights

* Lowest freight costs:

  * SP
  * PR
  * MG
  * RJ
  * DF

* Highest freight costs:

  * RR
  * PB
  * RO
  * AC
  * PI

* Fastest average deliveries:

  * SP
  * PR
  * MG
  * DF
  * SC

* Longest delivery times:

  * RR
  * AP
  * AM
  * AL
  * PA

### 💳 Payment Insights

* Credit cards were the most preferred payment method.
* Average credit card installment count was 3.51.
* Most customers preferred single-installment payments.
* Revenue showed continuous growth throughout the study period.

## Business Recommendations

### Marketing

* Schedule advertisements during peak shopping hours.
* Launch targeted campaigns in underpenetrated states.
* Promote evening flash-sale campaigns.

### Supply Chain

* Establish additional fulfillment centers near remote regions.
* Optimize freight routes for high-cost states.
* Improve delivery estimation accuracy.

### Payments

* Simplify installment selection during checkout.
* Promote installment plans for high-value purchases.
* Implement payment validation checks to improve data quality.
